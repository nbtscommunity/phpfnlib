<ul>Wow. I had a great time tonight. So I thought the peer helpers party was last night, and i was bummed because nobody called me, and i had to work, right? Well it's 6pm, and I'm sitting at my computer in my comfy pajama's, and Eric calls me, telling me that the party is tonight and started about 10 min ago. Only thing was, mom had left to go to the store, and I knew she'd be pissed if I left before she got back, especially since I never said anything about the party being tonight (due to not knowing about it...). I tell Eric that I have to wait for my mom, and he's all "well i got to go"... so i'm sitting here bummed, because I'm missing this party, right? Well mom gets home 5 min later, and erics already left. So i looked brandons number up, called and got directions.... and went! It rocked. There was only about 10 people, and we all left to rent movies, Scary Movie 2, and Blade..... and basically hung out watching movies, eating food, and talking. Brandons parents even watched scary movie 2 with us, hehehee. I didn't get home until 11:30pm, and mom was in a really good mood, and was like "glad you called when you got home, happy to hear you had a good time".
